## Application Process
1. Initial Application: Submit your resume and cover letter through our careers portal
2. Screening Call: 30-minute call with our HR team
3. Technical Interview: 1-hour interview with the hiring manager and team lead
4. Technical Assessment: Take-home project or coding exercise
5. Final Interviews: Panel interviews with team members and executives
6. Offer: Successful candidates will receive an offer within one week of final interviews
